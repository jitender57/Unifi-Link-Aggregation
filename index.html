UniFi - How to Enable Link Aggregation (LAG) on Switches

Link Aggregation (LAG) allows you to combine multiple network connections to increase bandwidth and provide redundancy. If you are using UniFi switches, you can enable LAG to improve your network performance. Follow these steps to configure LAG on your UniFi switches.

My Device List for This Setup –

  • UniFi Dream Machine Pro (UDM Pro)
  • UniFi Switch 24 PoE (USW-24-PoE)
  • UniFi Aggregation Switch (USW-Aggregation)
  • Two Direct Attach Copper (DAC) cables
  • A PC for accessing the UniFi Console

What Doesn't Work?

There are a number of things that are required for LAG to work and some things that UniFi doesn't support.

  • Ports must be sequential in number, and the same link speed.

For starters, the ports used must be sequential in number. For example, if you want four ports to be aggregated together, then on one switch you would have to use ports 5,6, 7 and 8. You can't use 1, 2, 7, and 8. On the other side of the LAG, it doesn't need to be the same ports - just any four in sequential order.

Another complication is that you can't mix link speeds. Even if the ports are in sequential order, you can't make a LAG with gigabit Ethernet ports and 10 Gbps SFP+ slots. You can combine 10 Gbps interfaces or 1 Gbps interfaces, but you can't mix them.

Step-by-Step Configuration

1. Connect Your Devices

  1. Connect your PC to the UDM Pro using an Ethernet cable.
  2. Log in to your UniFi Console.
  3. Take one DAC cable and connect:
  • One end to SFP Port 25 on the USW 24 PoE switch.
  • The other end to Port 5 on the USW Aggregation Switch.
  1. Take the second DAC cable and connect:
  • One end to SFP Port 26 on the USW 24 PoE switch.
  • The other end to Port 6 on the USW Aggregation Switch.

2. Configure Link Aggregation on USW 24 PoE

  1. Go to the UniFi Console.
  2. Navigate to the USW 24 PoE Switch.
  3. Select Port 25.
  4. Change the settings to Manual.
  5. Go to Operation Mode and switch from Switching to Aggregation.

3. Configure Link Aggregation on USW Aggregation Switch

  1. Go to the UniFi Console.
  2. Navigate to the USW Aggregation Switch.
  3. Select Port 5.
  4. Change the settings to Manual.
  5. Go to Operation Mode and switch from Switching to Aggregation.

Final Steps

Once you have completed these configurations, your two links will be aggregated, providing a 2 Gbps link speed between the USW 24 PoE switch and the USW Aggregation switch.

Enjoy improved network performance and resiliency and capacity with Link Aggregation!
